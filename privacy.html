Privacy Policy – TrunkDex

Last updated: 30 January 2026

TrunkDex respects your privacy. This application is designed for personal use and works without requiring user registration or accounts.

Information Collection and Use

TrunkDex does not collect, transmit, or store personal data on external servers. All data entered by the user, including tasting notes, ratings, photos, and locations, is stored locally on the user’s device.

Location Data

The application may access the device’s location only to allow users to save the location of tastings. Location data is stored locally on the device and is never transmitted to third parties.

Camera Access

Camera access is used solely for scanning barcodes and taking photos of spirits. All images are stored locally on the user’s device.

Internet Access

Internet access is used only to retrieve publicly available product information. No personal data is transmitted or shared.

Data Sharing

TrunkDex does not share user data with third parties.

Data Security

All data remains under the full control of the user and is stored locally on the device.

Children’s Privacy

This application is intended for users aged 16 and above.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page.
